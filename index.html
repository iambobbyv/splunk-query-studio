Getting Started

      


        

          What is Splunk Query Studio?
          

            
A desktop and mobile tool that helps SOC analysts and IT teams generate optimized Splunk SPL queries — without memorizing syntax. Pick a category, platform, and parameters and the app builds the query for you.

          

        


        

          How do I generate a query?
          

            
Follow the 4-step wizard: 1 pick a category (Network, Server, etc.), 2 select your platform, 3 fill in parameters (IP, time range, etc.), then 4 copy the generated SPL. Jump between completed steps using the progress bar at the top.

          

        


        

          How do I copy the SPL query?
          

            
On step 4, click Copy SPL in the header or results panel. Keyboard shortcut: Ctrl+Shift+C / ⌘+Shift+C.

          

        


        

          How do I start a new query?
          

            
Click New Query on step 4, or press Ctrl+Shift+N / ⌘+Shift+N from any step.

Queries & Presets

      


        

          The generated query looks wrong or incomplete
          

            
Queries are templates — you may need to adjust:

            
    
              
  • Index — replace index=* with your actual index
    • 
              
  • Sourcetype — match your Splunk sourcetype config
    • 
              
  • Field names — vary by data source and parsing rules
    • 
            

            
Use the AI Advisor to refine or explain specific commands.

          

        


        

          How do I save and reload presets?
          

            
On step 4, click Save Preset. Enter a name — the wizard state (category, platform, parameters, query) is saved in localStorage. Access presets in the right-hand panel under the Presets tab.

          

        


      


      
Platform & Compatibility

      


        

          Which Splunk versions are supported?
          

            
Splunk Enterprise 7.x and later, and Splunk Cloud. The app generates queries locally — paste them into Splunk's search bar.

          

        


        

          Does it work on iPhone, iPad, or mobile?
          

            
Yes. Fully responsive for iPhone and iPad with full-screen slide wizard, drawer sidebar, and bottom-sheet history panel. Tap ☰ for categories and the clock icon for history/AI.

          

        


        

          Does the app send data to the internet?
          

            
No. Fully offline. No queries, IPs, or usage data are ever transmitted. The AI only talks to localhost:11434. History and presets stay on your device.
